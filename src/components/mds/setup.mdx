## 评估指标
- 良性准确率 BA (Benign accuracy)
$$
\left \{
\begin{aligned}
    \text{BA} &= \frac{1}{M} \sum_{i=1}^{M} I\big(f_{\theta'}(x_i), y_i\big) \\
    \text{s.t. } &\; (x_i, y_i) \in \mathcal{D}_{test}
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
- 攻击成功率 ASR (Attack success rate)
$$
\left \{
\begin{aligned}
    \text{ASR} &= \frac{1}{M} \sum_{i=1}^{M} I\big(f_{\theta'}(x_i'), \ell_t\big) \\
    \text{s.t. } &\; (x_i, y_i) \in \mathcal{D}_{test}, \; y_i \neq \ell_t
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
